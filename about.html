🌈 About Queer Planet SMP

Queer Planet SMP is a survival Minecraft server created as a dedicated safe space for the LGBTQIA+ community, plural systems (both traumagenic and non-traumagenic), neurodivergent folks, and the people who love and support them.

We know how hard it can be to find spaces in gaming where you don’t have to hide who you are. Queer Planet SMP exists to change that — to offer a sanctuary where creativity, authenticity, and mutual respect thrive. Whether you’re here to build a mushroom cottage, survive with your systemmates, or just hang out in a cozy queer village, this is your home.

🛡️ What Makes Us Different

  • 18+ Only: Our server is strictly for adults — free from unsafe dynamics and age policing.
  • LGBTQIA+ Owned and Led: We’re not just inclusive — we’re queer-run from the ground up.
  • Plural-Inclusive and System-Safe: All systems are valid. No gatekeeping. No mockery.
  • Consent Culture in Practice: No PvP, teleporting, or pranks without clear and ongoing consent.
  • No Tolerance for Hate or Harassment: Bigotry is not welcome here — not even once.

🧱 Minecraft Server Features

  • 🌍 Survival-focused SMP gameplay
  • 🧭 Custom spawn & queer-themed zones
  • 🧙 Plural-friendly chat and private spaces
  • 📜 In-game rules and /report system
  • 💬 Discord-linked chat (real-time communication)
  • 🏠 Land claims, grief protection, and opt-in teleporting
  • 🏳️‍⚧️ Pride areas, memorials, and comfort builds

💬 Our Discord Community

Our Discord server is the heart of Queer Planet SMP — a 24/7 social space offering support, creativity, and connection.

  • 🪷 Safe venting and emotional support
  • 🎨 Art and self-expression spaces
  • 🔖 Plural check-ins and system threads
  • 🎉 Community events and game nights
  • 🎮 Cross-server chat and community polls

New members go through onboarding where they choose pronouns, identity roles, and support settings. Our mods are plural-aware and trauma-informed.

🏳️‍🌈 You Belong Here.

Queer Planet SMP is built on the belief that people flourish when they’re seen, respected, and celebrated — not just tolerated.

Whether you’re trans, ace, bi, aro, enby, questioning, plural, disabled, or all of the above — this world was built with you in mind. Come build something magical with us.
